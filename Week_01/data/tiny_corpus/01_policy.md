# Company Remote Work Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** October 15, 2024

## Overview

This policy outlines the guidelines and expectations for remote work arrangements at TechCorp Inc. Our goal is to provide flexibility while maintaining productivity, collaboration, and work-life balance.

## Eligibility

- All full-time employees with at least 6 months tenure are eligible
- Part-time employees may apply on a case-by-case basis
- Approval is at manager's discretion based on role requirements

## Work Schedule

- Core hours: 10 AM - 3 PM (local time) for team synchronization
- Flexible hours outside core time, with 8-hour daily commitment
- Must be available for meetings during core hours
- Advance notice required for schedule changes

## Equipment and Resources

### Company-Provided
- Laptop (MacBook Pro or equivalent)
- External monitor (upon request)
- Keyboard and mouse
- VPN access and security software

### Employee Responsibility
- Reliable high-speed internet (minimum 25 Mbps)
- Appropriate workspace with minimal distractions
- Maintain equipment in good condition

## Communication Expectations

- Respond to Slack messages within 2 hours during work hours
- Attend all scheduled team meetings via video
- Update calendar with availability and time off
- Use video during important meetings

## Security Requirements

- Use company VPN for all work activities
- Enable two-factor authentication on all accounts
- Do not share login credentials
- Lock computer when stepping away
- Report security incidents immediately

## Performance Metrics

Performance will be measured by:
- Quality and timeliness of deliverables
- Availability during core hours
- Collaboration and communication
- Meeting attendance and participation

## Travel and Office Visits

- Quarterly in-person team meetings required
- Travel expenses reimbursed per company policy
- Office space available for hot-desking when needed
- Advance booking required for on-site workspace

## Termination of Remote Work

Remote work privileges may be revoked if:
- Performance declines
- Communication issues arise
- Security policies are violated
- Business needs change

## Questions

Contact HR at hr@techcorp.com or your manager for clarification on any policy items.
